# Project Overview

StyleHub is a modern fashion and lifestyle e-commerce platform designed to provide customers with a seamless online shopping experience while enabling businesses to efficiently manage products, inventory, orders, and customer interactions.

The platform aims to bridge the gap between fashion retailers and customers through a secure, scalable, and user-friendly web application.

---

# Business Problem

Many small and medium fashion businesses struggle with:

- Managing products efficiently
- Handling customer orders
- Tracking inventory
- Processing payments securely
- Managing customer engagement

Customers also face challenges in discovering products, comparing options, and tracking orders.

StyleHub aims to solve these problems through a centralized digital platform.
